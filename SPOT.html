▶ how to use this

Setup

  1. Click start camera and allow access. Point the camera toward your spot — a small gap at the curtain edge is enough, even 1–2 cm.
  2. Click and drag on the video to draw a box tightly around just the parking space. Tighter zone = less noise = better accuracy.
  3. Make sure the spot is empty, then click capture empty spot. Stay still — it takes 5 frames over about a second and averages them.
  4. Click test beep to make sure your volume is up, then walk away. It'll beep when a parked car leaves.

Calibrating the threshold

  • Watch the Zone Diff meter. When the spot is empty it should read low (under 10%). When a car is there, it should jump higher (20–60%). Set the threshold slider between those two numbers.
  • False alarms from passing shadows or clouds → increase the threshold
  • Cars not being detected → decrease the threshold
  • Re-capture the reference if lighting changes significantly — the app adapts slowly but a big shift (day to night) needs a fresh capture
  • On a phone: prop it on the sill with just the lens peeking past the curtain
